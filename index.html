Shadman is a senior at George Mason University studying Information Technology with a minor in Business. Currently, he's most interested in the Blockchain and digital asset ecosystem. During the summer, he is an instructor at Mason Game & Tech Academy teaching programming courses that include Python, Java, Javascript, and Blockchain to incoming college students. ￼


Shadman's passion and research includes emerging technologies such as blockchain and smart contracts, artificial intelligence, and IoT. In 2018, he founded Blockchain at Mason, a student-run organization dedicated to blockchain education, research, and development at George Mason University. ￼


Shadman is the founder and CTO of Koinstreet, a social trading network with the vision of empowering everyday people by unlocking financial services that were historically only available to the 1%. Shadman is on a path of continuous self-improvement with a vision of making the world a better place through technology and education while being an advocate for a decentralized, distributed future and giving power back to the people.
